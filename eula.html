Petti Community

Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 4, 2026 · Contact: support@petti.online

Petti is a creative face painting community where users can explore official designs, share artwork, and generate AI-assisted face paint visuals.

These guidelines apply to all uploads, AI generations, comments, messages, profiles, reports, and community interactions on Petti.

1. Community Purpose

Petti is intended to support creative expression, festival inspiration, cosplay ideas, theatrical makeup, artistic face painting, cultural celebration, and respectful visual experimentation.

Users must follow these Community Guidelines in addition to Petti’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, threats, intimidation, humiliation, stalking, hateful conduct, targeted abuse, or malicious false reporting are prohibited.

Users may not attack or degrade individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, medical condition, age, immigration status, veteran status, or similar protected characteristics.

3. Face Photo Consent

Do not upload another person’s face photo without permission. This applies to friends, strangers, public figures, minors, customers, clients, models, and people appearing in group photos.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to upload and transform any image you submit to Petti. Content that violates privacy expectations may be removed.

4. AI Content Rules

AI-generated content must follow all community rules. Users may not use AI features to create abusive, hateful, sexual, violent, deceptive, impersonating, illegal, or privacy-invasive content.

Users may not attempt to bypass safety filters, manipulate the AI system, generate harmful outputs, or use AI-generated images to mislead others about identity, consent, affiliation, or real-world events.

5. Prohibited Content

  • Illegal, harmful, violent, threatening, or exploitative content.
  • Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • Sexually explicit, pornographic, exploitative, or non-consensual intimate content.
  • Graphic gore, extreme violence, or shock content.
  • Scams, phishing, impersonation, misleading promotions, or fake identities.
  • Content that exposes another person’s private information.
  • Copyright-infringing artwork, images, logos, or designs.

6. Artistic and Cultural Respect

Petti supports creative face painting, but users should be respectful when using designs inspired by cultures, ceremonies, symbols, communities, or traditions. Content that mocks, degrades, or targets protected groups may be removed.

Users should avoid presenting sensitive cultural, religious, or ceremonial designs in misleading or disrespectful ways.

7. Intellectual Property

Users should only upload photos, patterns, designs, logos, artwork, references, or creative materials that they own or are authorized to use.

Unauthorized use of copyrighted artwork, celebrity images, brand logos, third-party photography, or copied face paint designs may result in removal or account enforcement.

8. Safety and Cosmetic Disclaimer

Petti content is for digital visualization and inspiration. AI-generated designs do not guarantee real-world cosmetic safety or suitability.

Users should evaluate skin sensitivity, allergies, eye-area safety, product ingredients, and hygiene before applying any real face paint or makeup. Petti does not provide medical or dermatological advice.

9. Consumable Purchases

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, manipulate delivery, use unauthorized refunds, or use purchased items to harass or pressure others.

10. Reporting and Moderation

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, AI misuse, privacy violations, copyright infringement, impersonation, harassment, scams, or unsafe behavior.

Petti may review reports using automated tools, manual review, user reports, safety signals, and platform integrity systems.

11. Enforcement

Petti may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, generation restrictions, purchase restrictions, account suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

Severity, repetition, harm level, legal obligations, and App Store compliance requirements may affect enforcement decisions.

12. Contact

Questions about these guidelines may be sent to support@petti.online.
